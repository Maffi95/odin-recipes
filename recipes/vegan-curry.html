Vegan Curry

Description

Basic recipe for a healthy vegan curry. You can add basically any vegetables that fit your personal taste. Just try yourself out.

Ingredients

  • 600g vegetables e.g.
    • Carots
    • Bell-pepper
    • Cauliflower
    • Broccoli
    • Leek
  • 250g Red lentils
  • 1 can of coconut-milk
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 piece of ginger
  • 4 sticks of scallion
  • 1 tsp curry-paste
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces
  2. Cut the white part of the scallions into small slices
  3. Cut the garlic and ginger into small pieces (use an onion-chopper if you have one)
  4. Heat the oil in a large pan
  5. add the cutted scallions, ginger and garlic in the hot oil
  6. cook for about 2 minutes (should not get brown)
  7. add the curry-paste cook for another 2 minutes and stir frequently
  8. add the vegetables
  9. add some salt
  10. cook for 5-10 minutes on medium heat until the vegetables start browning
  11. add red lentils and coconut-milk
  12. cook until the lentils are soft and good to eat (usually about 15-20 minutes)
  13. add salt and pepper to taste
